Introduction

I have always enjoyed all things mechanical and robotic as long as I can remember. It has been a long journey, but I am currently a senior at Capitol Technology University studying mechatronics engineering. Along with the projects I complete at school, I enjoy building robots or other mechanical contraptions in my workshop. Education

I am a senior student at Capitol Technology University studying mechatronics engineering. Before attending Capitol Tech I received an associate degree in mechatronics engineering technology from Anne Arundel Community College (AACC). I had just received from AACC my associate degrees in web design and graphic design when I decided that engineering is what I wanted to pursue, so I made the switch and have no regrets. I graduated with my associate in mechatronic engineering technology from AACC with a 3.8 GPA and currently have a 3.9 GPA at Capitol Tech
